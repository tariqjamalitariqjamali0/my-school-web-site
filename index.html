About Dilpasand Mithai

Established in 1995, Dilpasand Mithai has been a beloved name in the community for over 25 years. Our commitment to quality and authentic recipes passed down through generations ensures every bite is a celebration of Pakistani culture and sweetness.

Our Popular Mithai

Kaju Barfi

Rich and creamy cashew nut fudge, delicately flavored and garnished with edible silver leaf.

Laddoo

Soft and sweet gram flour balls infused with cardamom and garnished with chopped pistachios.

Gulab Jamun

Soft, syrup-soaked milk dumplings with a fragrant rose and cardamom syrup.

Jalebi

Crispy, sweet, and sticky spirals soaked in saffron sugar syrup, perfect for any occasion.

Rasmalai

Soft cheese patties soaked in sweetened saffron milk, garnished with nuts.

Besan Barfi

Traditional gram flour fudge with a nutty flavor and a smooth texture.
